Who is Karthik?

Karthik belongs to a very specific group of people who are uniquely artistic and extremely analytical at the same time. You could say that the left and right halves of his brain are in perfect harmony. This unique combination of order and chaos has enabled him to become a master at problem identification and pattern matching.

His academic background includes a Bachelors degree in Computer Science from the prestigious IIT Bombay in India, admission into which is not a simple matter. He gained entrance to this world renowned institute after securing an All India Rank of 83 in one of the most difficult technical exams in the world, IITJEE, in the year 2010 which was given by approximately half a million aspiring students.

After graduating from IIT Bombay in 2014, he went on to join Northeastern University, located in Boston, for his Masters degree in Computer Science. Though he chose to do a specialization in Game Development, his technical background and well as his Co-op experience at PayPal make him ready for any role in the spectrum of Software Development stack.

He has experience in Front-End development using such technologies as AngularJS, Bootstrap and Angular Material for styling and jQuery, VelocityJS and JavaScript for animations. He also has experience in Back-End development using such technologies as NodeJS and ExpressJS for server side programming and MongoDB using MongooseJS for database access and usage.

He is quite proficient in various programming languages, Java being his go-to language of choice. He is recently discovering the pleasure of using JavaScript while learning the intricacies of the MEAN stack. He wishes to, at least in the near future, be proficient in JavaScript as well.
